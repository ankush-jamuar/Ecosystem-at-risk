What could be done
for conservation?

  • Establish protected areas to safeguard critical habitats and species.

  • Implement sustainable land-use practices to minimize habitat destruction.

  • Promote responsible consumption to reduce resource depletion.

  • Enforce strict regulations against illegal logging, poaching, and pollution.

  • Support community-based conservation initiatives for local empowerment and involvement.

  • Educate the public about the importance of biodiversity and ecosystem services.

  • Invest in research to understand ecosystem dynamics and inform conservation efforts.

  • Foster international cooperation for transboundary conservation of shared ecosystems.

  • Restore degraded ecosystems through reforestation, habitat restoration, and species reintroduction.

  • Integrate conservation into development plans for balanced human-nature interactions.
